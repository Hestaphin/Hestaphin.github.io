Learning at PUCO

Music theory, guided listening, and practical ways into repertoire.

The Learn page expands PUCO’s educational work for members, audiences, and curious beginners. It turns chamber music from something admired at a distance into something understood and enjoyed from the inside.

Learning formats

How we teach

  • Short theory sessions linked directly to repertoire.
  • Listening guides for concerts and recordings.
  • Peer-led workshops for student musicians.

Music Theory Lessons

Compact lessons on harmony, form, rhythm, texture, and ensemble listening, designed to connect musical concepts with real pieces.

Listening Guides

Annotated listening companions that help audiences notice structure, gesture, and instrumental colour.

Student Workshops

Interactive sessions where members discuss repertoire, rehearsal craft, and communication across sections.

Learning at PUCO is not separate from performance. It is one of the ways performance becomes more generous, more precise, and more memorable.
