About the orchestra

A chamber orchestra platform built around artistry, curiosity, and shared making.

PUCO was conceived not just as an ensemble, but as a flexible structure for performance, learning, and musical community. It values refined musicianship while staying open, student-driven, and collaborative.

Core values

What guides us

  • Artistic seriousness without exclusivity.
  • Collaborative work across performers and organisers.
  • Learning as part of the public musical experience.

PUCO aims to make chamber music feel alive in the present: attentive in craft, warm in tone, and open in invitation.
