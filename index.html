Meet PUCO

About PUCO

An orchestra platform for performance, learning, and musical community.

Peking Unnamed Chamber Orchestra is designed as a flexible musical home: part ensemble, part creative platform, and part educational space. We present intimate performances, create room for young musicians to develop, and invite audiences to experience chamber music in a more human and connected way.

Our work is rooted in collaboration. Players, organisers, supporters, and listeners all help shape the season, whether through rehearsals, workshops, event planning, or patron support.

We organise music theory sessions, listening guides, and accessible ways into repertoire.

PUCO’s learning section is for anyone who wants to understand music more deeply, whether you are a performer, a first-time listener, or someone returning to classical music with fresh ears.
